Our Story

A coaching built on results & trust

Pioneer Coaching was started in Bettiah with one simple goal — give students of Class 11 & 12 the kind of focused, high-quality teaching in Mathematics and Biology that usually requires moving to a big city.

Over the years we've grown into one of the area's most trusted names for board preparation. Our approach is simple: strong fundamentals, consistent practice, honest feedback, and a teacher who genuinely knows every student in the room.

  • Concept-first teaching

    We build understanding before speed — so marks come naturally.

  • Accountability

    Weekly tests and parent updates keep everyone on track.

  • Personal attention

    Small batches mean no student is ever just a roll number.
